Who We Are

Granix Prime Stones Ltd is a leading supplier of premium natural stones based in Kampala, Uganda, catering to both local and international markets. We specialize in providing high-quality granite, marble, and quartz for diverse applications.

  • Applications: Kitchens, bathrooms, floor tiles, wall cladding, staircases, walkways, monuments
